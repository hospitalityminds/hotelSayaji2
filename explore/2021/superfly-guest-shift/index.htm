    SUPERFLY X The Odd Couple : Guest Shift

    20 Aug 2020 | The Opposite House

    Share

    SUPERFLY : Terrace Opening Party

    SUPERFLY is partnering up with PEDDLERS GIN to launch the first SUPERFLY Guest Shift, inviting Satoshi the Bar Manager of The Odd Couple in Shanghai, ranked No, 38 of Asia's 50 best bars 2020.

     

    Satoshi

    His bar industry career started at the age of 20, followed with 9 years of learning and training, focusing on classic Japanese and flair bartending in Tokyo. Satoshi achieved notable awards and participated in many bar industry events, he then moved to Shanghai, spending over a year as Bar manager at The Odd Couple since opening.

     

    The Odd Couple

    Hidden away in chic Xintiandi, this is a cozy little upstairs bar. It's a joint venture between Shingo Gokan from "Speak Low" in Shanghai and Tokyo, and Steve Schneider of "Employees Only", who are long-time buddies but with very different bartending styles, which give the bar its name. These have been combined into a fun, bright and kitschy 80s tribute bar.

     

    Peddlers Gin Company

    The first craft gin from China, Peddlers award-winning gin offers rare notes of Sichuan pepper, Buddha's hand and lotus leaves for a distinctive flavor unlike any other. Handcrafted in China for discerning and adventurous drinkers.
