    Frasca

    Regional Italian cooking with soul

    CUISINE

    Italian

    LOCATION

    Level 1,The Opposite House, Taikoo Li Sanlitun North, No. 11 Sanlitun Road, Chaoyang District, Beijing, China 100027

    HOURS

    Breakfast 6:30am – 10:30am
    Lunch 12pm – 2:30pm
    Dinner 6pm – 10:30pm

    CONTACT

    (+) 86 10 6410 5210 frasca@theoppositehouse.com

    Frasca is a casual Italian eatery with an inviting dining room and terrace. Named after the symbolic branches used to welcome passers-by in Northeast Italy, it pays homage to authentic regional cooking rooted in traditional family recipes. Frasca's cooking style reflects its Italian heritage, passion for wholesome ingredients, time-honoured techniques and heart-warming dishes.
